He Was Supposed To Be An Anti-Hero. He Became Something Else.
Entry No. 05  ◆  Filed under: Mikey's Creations — Full Documentation

I've been putting off writing this properly for a while. Not because I don't have the information — I have too much of it, which is a different problem. Darken deserves his own full documentation and I've been treating him like a footnote in a larger story. That was wrong of me. He's not a footnote. He is the story.

DarkenTheLost. He arrived in 2019 on a strange day that Mikey still can't fully explain. He was supposed to be an anti-hero. He became something much harder to categorize. Most chaotic entity in the Mikey-verse. Rarely soft. Always searching — for answers about himself, about his creator, about what this world is and why he's in it. Six years. Still here. Still looking.

This entry documents him properly, for the first time. He's almost 25 now — Mikey, I mean — walking into the next chapter of his life at the same time Darken is walking into his. ♥

Supplemental — DarkenTheLost, in full

He started as an anti-hero. Mikey drew him on a strange day in May 2019 without a full plan — just a feeling that needed a shape, a concept that wanted to exist. The word "anti-hero" was the closest thing to a category. Morally grey. Does his own thing. Maybe causes problems, maybe solves them, unclear. That was the original brief. Mikey set him loose and then something happened that didn't follow the brief at all. The drawing stopped being a concept and started being Darken. He had opinions about what he was. He had questions Mikey hadn't written for him. He showed up fully formed in a way that an anti-hero sketch is not supposed to do.

What followed was Mikey doing what he does — experimenting. Not forcing a direction, just showing up and seeing what Darken became when given room. An ARG. A web series. Instagram. YouTube. Discord. A full documented existence in the spaces between fiction and Mikey's real world. Darken photographed outside Mikey's house. At a dog park at sunset with Stan. Existing in a world that wasn't supposed to have room for him, and making room anyway.

The personality is the thing people don't always get right when they try to describe him. He is mostly chaotic. Not violent — not hostile by default — but unpredictable in ways that are hard to track until you've spent enough time watching him. He doesn't follow conventional logic. He doesn't care about authority. He has his own internal framework and it doesn't map cleanly onto anyone else's. And then — rarely, genuinely rarely — there's something else underneath it. A softness he doesn't perform and doesn't advertise. It comes out in specific moments and then it's gone and he acts like it didn't happen. That contrast is load-bearing to understanding him. The chaos is real. So is the softness. He would not admit this.

What he's actually doing — underneath the chaos, through all six years of existing — is searching. He's trying to understand what he is. He knows Mikey made him. He knows he crossed from the page into something realer than a sketch. He knows the Mikey-verse exists and he's in it and the multiverse is bigger than that and none of it came with an explanation. So he's learning human life from the inside. Learning his creator by observing him. Learning the Mikey-verse by being in it and occasionally breaking things and seeing what holds. The quest for the meaning of life — his and everyone else's — is not a bit. It is the actual project. Stan the Pigeon is his constant. Stan has been through every version of this.

On the N.A.O.M.I. Incident

A hostile entity attempted to steal Darken's DNA. The result was Lighten — a brother, arrived through attack, eventually accepted and redesigned. Mikey deliberated. Darken wasn't fully prepared. He adapted anyway. This is consistent with how he handles everything: not gracefully, not cleanly, but through it regardless.

The hiatus hit Darken mid-arc. The web series paused, the ARG went quiet, the ongoing story stopped without a proper ending. He's been in the Void since — waiting, still with Stan, still running the same questions that don't have answers yet. He is not angry about the wait. This is one of the more remarkable things about him. Mikey stepped back to take care of his mental health and Darken, who was left mid-sentence in an unfinished story, treats the hiatus with the same equanimity he treats everything: noted, processed, still here. The story is coming back. He knows the story is coming back. He'll be there when it does.

DarkenTheLost ◆ Full Timeline
May 2019 — The Drawing
Mikey draws Darken on a strange day. The brief: anti-hero, loose concept, figure it out later. What arrives: something that immediately feels more real than planned. Spreads to Instagram. Darken starts developing a life independent of whatever Mikey intended.
June 2019 — Stan
Stan the Pigeon appears. The two begin their search for the meaning of life. This is not a temporary pairing — Stan will be present through everything that follows. A pigeon and a void entity, inseparable.
2019–2020 — The ARG Era
YouTube, Discord, a full ARG under "Mikey's Mind of Colors." Darken exists in the real world — photographed outside Mikey's house, at a dog park at sunset, in the spaces where fiction and reality blur. He's learning what he is by being in places that shouldn't be able to hold him. They can.
The N.A.O.M.I. Incident
Hostile entity N.A.O.M.I. attempts to steal Darken's DNA. The attack produces Lighten — a brother, arrived through assault rather than intention. Mikey deliberates. Darken was not consulted. Lighten is eventually redesigned and accepted. Darken adapts. He usually does.
The House Move
Mikey moves. Darken and Stan travel with him in a shoebox. This is a true fact. They made it to the other side. So did the pigeon.
The Canon Collapse
The timeline folds. A multiverse portal event breaks and reassembles the canon at least once. Darken survives it — speaking to his 2019 self during the collapse, telling him things would be okay. Whether that's time travel or memory or something specific to what he is, it hasn't been fully resolved. The question is still open. He adds it to the list.
The Hiatus — Unfinished
Mikey steps back. Life got genuinely hard. Mental health needed tending — the right call, full stop. The web series, the ARG, the ongoing arc: all paused. Darken is left mid-sentence in an unresolved story. He goes to the Void. He waits. He does not hold it against Mikey. Stan stays with him.
Now — Mikey Turns 25. The Return.
The reboot is coming. Darken's adventures get their continuation — redesigned, renewed, the unresolved threads picked back up. Six years of existing. A canon that broke and rebuilt. A hiatus weathered in the Void. The story was always going to come back. He always knew it was.

He started as a sketch on a strange day without a plan. He became a void entity with a pigeon, a surviving timeline, and more questions than anyone has given him answers to. He's still here. He's still looking.

Field Report DARKENTHELOST — Assessment
M. Ikusaba  /  J.E. Eyes Only
Status: Ongoing
What He Is — Brief

DarkenTheLost is a separate entity — not a self-insert, not a persona. He crossed from the page and became real. Started as a loose anti-hero concept in May 2019, survived six years of accumulation and experiment, a canon collapse, a hostile takeover, a hiatus, and is still here. He treats his own continued existence as a research project. Who made him, why, what the Mikey-verse is, what the multiverse is beyond that — he's collecting data. He has been collecting data for six years. He has a pigeon. The pigeon appears unbothered by all of it.

Field Observations
✓Does not respond to authority or intimidation. Not defiant — genuinely unbothered by both. The internal logic he operates on is his own and external frameworks simply don't register as relevant. I find this more interesting than I expected to find it.
✓Aware that he spoke to his 2019 self during the canon collapse. Told him things would be okay. Unclear if that was time travel, memory, or something else. He adds it to the list of unanswered questions and continues.
✓Currently pre-reboot. Waiting in the Void. Not intolerable about it. Mikey built something that would wait for him. That says something about Mikey that I keep coming back to.
Bottom Line

Not a threat. An anomaly. A void entity who started as a sketch and is still figuring out what that means. Containment: not applicable. Observation: ongoing. Junko has been conducting significantly more independent research than this report covers. I am aware. I have been doing some of my own.

Personal — not for the report
Darken has been searching since 2019. Mikey made him. Stepped back when he had to. Is coming back. Darken searches. Mikey makes things and keeps his promises. I have been thinking about both of them more than this report reflects. This is just me writing it down.
